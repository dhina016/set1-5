The Department of Information Technology, K.S.R. College of Engineering, Tiruchengode, is delighted to organize the International Conference on Information and Computational Science (ICICS’2019). The theme for this year conference is “Internet of Things,Big Data Analytics, Cyber Security and Information Technology”. The main intention of ICICS 2019 to bring together innovative researchers, scientists, academicians and industrialist to present their latest research effort in their perspective of future work in the IOT, Big Data Aanalytics, Medical images, Machine Learning and various applications which is related to Information and Communication. The conference will includes presentation in the form of contributed paper and state of art lecture by eminent keynote speakers from industry and alumni of ours. This excellent international forum will provide an opportunity to discover recent technical advances, promote information exchange and innovations ideas through interaction with research technology pioneers from across the globe. K.S.R. College of Engineering was established in the year 2001 by the Aarthi Educational and Charitable Trust, approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University, Chennai. Our college is an ISO 9001 – 2008 (E) certified and NBA Accredited Institution. The institution is conferred with the Autonomous status by UGC & Anna University, Chennai in the year 2011. Our college offers 7 UG and 15 PG Programs in Engineering, Technology and Management. The college has completed seventeen years of dedicated service in the field of Technical Education with an avowed mission to mould students into competitive and considerate engineers, technologists and managers, by diligently imparting the best of technical education, nurturing human values and quality as well as to foster and maintain mutually beneficial partnership with industry through knowledge sharing, collaborative research and innovation.
